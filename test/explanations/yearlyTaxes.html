Yearly Taxes

Definition: The total yearly property taxes owed.
How it Applies: Yearly Taxes are subtracted from Yearly Rent (along with other recurring costs) to determine net yearly rent.

Yearly Rent = (Monthly Rent * 12)
Yearly Rent - (recurring costs) = Net Yearly Rent
Net Yearly Rent/Initial Investment = Yield
Tips: The property taxes are typically available on local municipal websites. Not all tax rates are the same, so determining the local rate on a potential investment is important. Depending on the region, tax abatements (or other tax reduction programs) may be available, which can affect the Yearly Tax.

If you can't find the Yearly Tax anywhere, use 1% of the total purchase price as an estimate.
